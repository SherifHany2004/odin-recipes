Lasagna

Description

A delicious Italian dish with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles until tender.
  2. Cook the ground beef and mix with tomato sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 45 minutes.
