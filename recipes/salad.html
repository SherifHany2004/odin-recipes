Fresh Garden Salad

Description

A healthy mix of crisp vegetables and a simple homemade dressing.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice

Steps

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. Combine them in a bowl.
  3. Mix olive oil and lemon juice for dressing.
  4. Toss salad with dressing and serve fresh.
