Homemade Pizza

Description

A crispy homemade pizza topped with cheese, sauce, and your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the dough on a flat surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly.
  4. Add cheese and toppings.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes until golden brown.
